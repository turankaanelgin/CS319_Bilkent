It is straightforward to play and enjoyable. In the game, there is a ball sequence moving in a maze, which is composed of different colors of balls and players' purpose is to destroy the sequence by making combinations of balls with same colors. For this purpose, they control a shooter which throws the balls from the middle of the screen. If they are not able to destroy the sequence on time, it will go through a hole and they lose. Every successful trial helps players to finish the level faster and their scores will be recorded according to the times they finish the level. Sometimes a coin appears in the corner of the screen and it is a gift for players for purchasing some special items from the store of the game. The aim is to play all levels successfully and complete them as much as fast.
